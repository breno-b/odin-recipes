Baked Ham

Description

Sweetly seasoned ham roasted with moist heat is a delicious way to celebrate special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Ham

  • Cloves

  • 1 inch of water

Steps

  1. Press cloves into the top of the ham. Pack with brown sugar.

  2. Add water to the roasting pan.

  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the ham is done.
